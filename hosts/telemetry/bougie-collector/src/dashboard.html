Hit a bug? bougie diagnose assembles a rich report from your last failure — it shows you the full payload and sends nothing without your explicit confirmation — then hands you a report id to paste into a GitHub issue. It works even with telemetry off.

Turn telemetry off any time: bougie telemetry off · honor guaranteed for DO_NOT_TRACK · inspect your own queue with bougie telemetry log · data: /data.json
